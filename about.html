About

In Two Design was founded in 2012 on the idea that transforming a space should have the power to transform a person. We strive to give our clients that "wow" moment that only a well-designed space can elicit.

Our goal for every project is to provide our clients with inventive solutions and exceptional customer service. By understanding our clients' personal desires, needs, and unique characters, we are able to create custom designs that are both beautiful and functional.

Company owner and ASID Designer Sari Sunshine and Design Assistant Lyssa Goldberg have a great deal of experience in a range of services, including:

  • Residential and commercial interior design
  • Use-what-you-have service
  • Custom furniture
  • Reupholstery
  • Procurement
  • Renovation
  • Installation
  • Floorplans
  • Specifying
